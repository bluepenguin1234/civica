The problem

Zillow tells you the price.
Nobody tells you if the city or town works.

You're about to make one of the most important financial decisions of your life. Your agent doesn't know if the city or town is fiscally solvent. School ranking sites don't show the 10-year trend. And you won't know the real tax burden until after you close.

🏦

Is the city or town fiscally healthy?

Free cash, bond rating, pension liability, debt per capita — none of this appears on a listing. Civica puts it front and center.

🎓

Are the schools trending up or down?

A school ranked #150 that was #200 last year is a buy. One that fell from #80 to #150 is a red flag. Ranks without trajectory are useless.

💰

What's the real cost to own?

Median tax bill, effective rate, municipal electric savings — the total cost of ownership can swing $3,000/year between neighboring cities and towns.
