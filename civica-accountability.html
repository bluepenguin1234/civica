Methodology
How Flags Are Calculated
Every flag uses public data and objective thresholds. No editorial judgment is applied to individual municipalities. Thresholds are based on MA state guidance, actuarial standards, and peer analysis within the 36-town dataset.
🏦
Pension Funding Level
Source: MA PERAC actuarial valuations. Most Essex County towns are in the Essex Regional Retirement System (~53.8% funded). Cities with independent systems (Lynn, Lawrence, Haverhill, Methuen) are separately valued.

Critical below 55% · Warning 55–70%

Below 55%, unfunded liabilities typically require significant future tax increases to resolve.
Coverage: 36 / 36 towns
💵
Free Cash Reserves
Source: MA Division of Local Services (DLS) certified free cash — the prior year's operating surplus, certified by the state each fall.

Critical below 2% · Warning 2–3%

MA DLS recommends at least 5% as a fiscal buffer. Below 3% means almost no cushion for unexpected costs or revenue shortfalls.
Coverage: ~22 / 36 towns
🎓
Academic Return on Investment
Compares per-pupil spending rank vs. academic outcome rank (DESE, 351 districts) within the 36-town peer group. Formula: gap = spend_pct − outcome_pct.

Critical gap >30 pts · Warning gap 15–30 pts

A large gap means a town spends more than peers yet achieves lower outcomes. Note: district size and demographics affect per-pupil costs.
Coverage: 34 / 36 towns
📊
Per-Capita Debt Load
Source: Town ACFRs and MA DLS gateway. Includes general obligation bonds. High debt/capita is not inherently negative — it can reflect capital investment — but it constrains future budgets and raises debt service costs.

Critical above $7,000/resident · Warning $4,500–$7,000

MA median is approximately $2,400 per capita.
Coverage: ~12 / 36 towns
Data Sources
Where the Numbers Come From
All data sourced from public records. Confidence levels for each municipality are shown in the main Civica scorecard.
MA PERAC
Annual actuarial valuations for all public pension systems
MA DLS Gateway
Certified free cash, debt schedules, municipal finance
MA DESE
District rankings, per-pupil spending, MCAS, grad rates
EMMA / MSRB
Bond ratings and official municipal disclosures
FBI UCR / MA EOPSS
Violent and property crime rates per 100k residents
Census ACS 2023
Population, income, poverty, education attainment
Important: Civica flags are statistical screening tools, not audit findings. A flag does not indicate fraud, waste, or abuse — it identifies a data point that deviates from peer averages or established thresholds and warrants public attention. Many flags reflect structural factors (regional pension systems, small-district scale effects, capital investment cycles) rather than management failures. Some data is estimated where primary sources were inaccessible. This tool is for informational purposes only. Civica is not affiliated with any government entity.
