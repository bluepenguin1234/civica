Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 12, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 12, 2026

Overview

Civica is a free public-information tool. We do not require accounts, we do not sell your data, and we do not build advertising profiles. This page explains what we collect, why, and how you can control it.

Information we collect

Email address (optional). If you submit the "Notify Me" form, we collect your email address to send product updates. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

Analytics (if enabled). We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic — which town profiles are viewed most, where visitors come from, and how the site is used. Analytics data is anonymized and not linked to individual identities. If you have a browser extension that blocks analytics, it will work fine.

Server logs. Like all websites, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may record IP addresses, browser types, and request timestamps in standard server logs. We do not access or use these logs for tracking.

What we do not collect

  • No accounts or passwords
  • No payment or financial information
  • No location data (we don't request it)
  • No cookies beyond what Google Analytics sets
  • No cross-site tracking or third-party advertising pixels

How we use your information

  • Email address — to send product update emails you explicitly requested. Never sold or shared with third parties.
  • Analytics data — to improve the product: understand which town profiles are useful, find broken flows, and prioritize features.

Third-party services

Civica uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • GitHub Pages — website hosting
  • Google Analytics — aggregate traffic analytics
  • Formspree — email form submission processing

We do not embed social media widgets, ad networks, or tracking pixels from any other third parties.

Data about municipalities

All municipal data displayed on Civica (fiscal metrics, school rankings, tax rates, crime statistics, etc.) is sourced from public government records. This is public information — it is not personal data and is not subject to deletion requests.

Sources include the Massachusetts Department of Revenue Division of Local Services, MA PERAC, DESE, FBI UCR, and the U.S. Census Bureau. Each data point on the site includes a citation to its source.

Your rights

If you submitted your email address and want it removed from our list, email us at hello@civica.com and we will delete it promptly. Because we don't create user accounts, there is no other personal profile to delete.

Children

Civica is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Civica after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@civica.com.
