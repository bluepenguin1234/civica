The problem

Zillow tells you the price.
Nobody tells you if the town works.

You're about to spend $600k+. Your agent doesn't know if the town is fiscally solvent. School ranking sites don't show the 10-year trend. And you won't know the real tax burden until after you close.

🏦

Is the town fiscally healthy?

Free cash, bond rating, pension liability, debt per capita — none of this appears on a listing. Civica puts it front and center.

🎓

Are the schools trending up or down?

A school ranked #150 that was #200 last year is a buy. One that fell from #80 to #150 is a red flag. Ranks without trajectory are useless.

💰

What's the real cost to own?

Median tax bill, effective rate, municipal electric savings — the total cost of ownership can swing $3,000/year between neighboring towns.
